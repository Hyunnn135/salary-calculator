What is Weekly Holiday Pay?

If you work 15+ hours per week and have perfect attendance, you receive an extra day's pay. This applies equally to part-time workers.

Minimum Wage Violation

Paying less than minimum wage (10,320 KRW) is punishable by up to 3 years imprisonment or a fine of up to 20 million KRW. The Minimum Wage Act applies equally to full-time, contract, and part-time workers.

During a probation period, the minimum wage can be reduced to 90% for up to 3 months. (Excludes contracts shorter than 1 year)

FAQ

What is the 2026 minimum wage?
The 2026 minimum wage is 10,320 KRW per hour. Based on 209 hours/month, the monthly equivalent is 2,156,880 KRW.
What should I do if I'm paid below minimum wage?
This violates the Minimum Wage Act, punishable by up to 3 years imprisonment or a 20 million KRW fine. You can report it through the Ministry of Employment and Labor hotline (1350) or their online reporting center.
Does minimum wage apply during probation?
For contracts of 1 year or longer, employers may pay 90% of minimum wage (9,288 KRW) for up to 3 months of probation. However, simple labor positions are exempt from this reduction.
What is the effective hourly wage including weekly holiday pay?
Weekly holiday pay is added when working 15+ hours/week. For a 5-day work week, the effective hourly wage is 10,320 × 1.2 = approximately 12,384 KRW.
Are meal and transportation allowances included in the wage?
Cash meal and transportation allowances have specific rules for inclusion in minimum wage calculations. Regularly paid meal allowances are included within certain limits.
Do part-time workers need social insurance?
Workers with 15+ hours/week must enroll in all 4 social insurance programs. Short-term workers (under 15 hours/week) may be exempt from National Pension and Employment Insurance.

2026 Minimum Wage Complete Guide

The 2026 minimum wage has been set at 10,320 KRW. This is a 170 KRW (1.7%) increase from the previous year's 10,150 KRW. Based on a 40-hour work week, the monthly equivalent (209 hours) is 2,156,880 KRW, and the annual equivalent is approximately 25,882,560 KRW.

Scope of Minimum Wage Application

The minimum wage applies to all businesses employing one or more workers. It applies equally regardless of employment type — full-time, contract, or part-time — and also applies to foreign workers. However, probationary employees may receive 90% of the minimum wage for up to 3 months from the start of probation. This reduction does not apply to contracts shorter than 1 year.

Penalties for Minimum Wage Violations

Under Article 28 of the Minimum Wage Act, employers who pay less than minimum wage face up to 3 years imprisonment or a fine of up to 20 million KRW. If you suspect a violation, you can report it to the Ministry of Employment and Labor (hotline: 1350) or file a complaint at the local labor office.

Minimum Wage Trends Over the Past 5 Years

2022 9,160 KRW → 2023 9,620 KRW(5.0% increase) → 2024: 9,860 KRW (2.5%) → 2025: 10,150 KRW (2.9%) → 2026: 10,320 KRW (1.7%). The rate of increase has been gradually slowing in recent years, reflecting the Minimum Wage Commission's consideration of economic conditions and business capacity. The minimum wage is announced by August 5 each year and takes effect January 1 of the following year.

Wage Components Included in Minimum Wage

It's important to understand which wage components count toward minimum wage. Wages paid regularly at least once a month are included, covering not just base pay but also fixed allowances. However, overtime, night, and holiday premiums, unused annual leave pay, family allowances, and commuting allowances are excluded even if paid monthly. Bonuses exceeding 5% of the monthly equivalent and welfare benefits (meals, transportation, lodging) exceeding 1% are included. When checking if your pay meets minimum wage, consider these inclusion rules for accurate comparison.
