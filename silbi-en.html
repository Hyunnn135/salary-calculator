Insurance Generation별 차이

Gen 1(2009년 이전): 급여 10%, Nearly full coverage for non-covered items. Most favorable but premiums may keep rising.

Gen 2(2009~2017): 급여 10%, 비급여 20% 자기부담. Gen 1보다 보장이 줄었지0K 여전히 양호합니다.

Gen 3(2017~2021): 급여 20%, 비급여 특약 분리. 비Covered Co-pay이 30%로 늘어났습니다.

Gen 4(2021년 이후): 급여 20%, 30% non-covered + usage management system (discount/surcharge). Lowest premiums but highest co-pay.

FAQ

Q. How can I find out what generation my insurance is?
A. Check your policy enrollment date. Before Oct 2009 = Gen 1, before Apr 2017 = Gen 2, before Jul 2021 = Gen 3, after = Gen 4.
Q. What are non-covered treatments?
A. Medical services not covered by National Health Insurance. Common examples include manual therapy, MRI, premium ward fees, and non-covered injections.
Q. Gen 4 discount/surcharge system?
A. Based on your non-covered expenses over the past year, next year's premiums are discounted (low usage) or surcharged (high usage).
